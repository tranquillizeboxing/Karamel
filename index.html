A brief intro.

We call ourselves KARAMEL. Also known as Voicebeat productions. Established in 2011, we provide a range of wedding services at your disposal. Never expect anything less as we guarantee high quality services for your BIG day! On top of that, we do events such as community centre shows as well as corporate DnD and many more. With our vast experience, be sure to create KARAMEL memories!
